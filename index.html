Ilana Glazer

  • 1987 - Born in New York City, New York
  • 2006 - Takes improv classes at the Upright Citizens Brigade Theatre and performs around New York City
  • 2009 - Co-creates the comedy web series “Broad City” with Abbi Jacobson and receives attention from The Wall Street Journal and Entertainment Weekly
  • 2011 - With comedian Amy Poehler as producer, Ilana and Abbi get script approval from Comedy Central.
  • 2014 - Broad City broadcasts in January and receives strong ratings and positive reviews
  • 2015 to present - Ilana is involved in activism, using her platform to further highlight racial and social justice.

"I miss improv. I hate it in a way - watching it, doing it - but only because it's so challenging and nerve wracking. Improv is the only belief system I've ever experienced that directly works on how to be. Just how to be." Ilana Glazer
